Fixed State Machine
I was tasked with making a self-automated watering system

S1: water sensor of max capacity of the water tank
S2: water sensor of almost full capacity
SH2: water in the main pipe while watering
SH1: humidity sensor of the garden
SC: rain sensor
MCA: water in the well
M: motor that fills the tank
V: water gate of the main pipe

This machine is able of watering a garden with a sealed tank while refiling without overflow, has flags for erros that might occur and the SOLVE option to lead of where it was detected, can be optimize with: the ammount of water nedded, flow of the refil engine, water gate - water sensor of the main pipe distance.
